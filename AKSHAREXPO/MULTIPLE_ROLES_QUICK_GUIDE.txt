    MULTIPLE ROLES SELECTION - QUICK GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✅ FEATURE: Users can now select MULTIPLE roles during registration!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
   BEFORE vs AFTER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BEFORE (Single Selection):
  ○ Job Seeker     (Radio button - can only pick ONE)
  ○ Recruiter
  ○ Mentor
  ○ Trainer

AFTER (Multiple Selection):
  ☐ Job Seeker     (Checkboxes - can pick MULTIPLE)
  ☑ Recruiter
  ☑ Mentor
  ☐ Trainer

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
   WHAT CHANGED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. ✅ Radio buttons → Checkboxes
2. ✅ name="role" → name="roles"
3. ✅ Shows ALL selected role-specific fields
4. ✅ Displays count: "Selected Roles (3)"
5. ✅ Saves to multiple Google Sheets
6. ✅ Validation: Must select at least 1 role

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
   EXAMPLE USAGE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SCENARIO 1: Job Seeker Who Also Mentors
  ☑ Job Seeker
  ☑ Mentor
  ☐ Trainer
  
  Result: "Selected Roles (2): 🔍 Job Seeker, 🎓 Mentor"
  Shows: Job Seeker fields + Mentor fields
  Saved to: Main sheet + Job_Seekers sheet + Mentors sheet

SCENARIO 2: Versatile Professional
  ☑ Job Seeker
  ☑ Mentor
  ☑ Trainer
  ☑ Consultant
  
  Result: "Selected Roles (4): 🔍 Job Seeker, 🎓 Mentor, 👨‍🏫 Trainer, 📊 Consultant"
  Shows: All 4 role-specific field sections
  Saved to: Main sheet + 4 role-specific sheets

SCENARIO 3: Focused User
  ☑ Job Seeker
  ☐ Recruiter
  ☐ Mentor
  
  Result: "Selected Roles (1): 🔍 Job Seeker"
  Shows: Only Job Seeker fields
  Saved to: Main sheet + Job_Seekers sheet

   HOW TO TEST
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: Clear Browser Cache
  Windows: Ctrl + Shift + Delete
  Mac: Cmd + Shift + Delete

STEP 2: Open Registration Form
  URL: https://www.aksharjobs.com/AKSHAREXPO/registration.html

STEP 3: Select Multiple Roles
  ✅ Click "Job Seeker" checkbox
  ✅ Click "Mentor" checkbox
  ✅ Click "Trainer" checkbox

STEP 4: Verify Display
  Should show: "✓ Selected Roles (3):"
  Should show: Three role cards with icons
  Should show: All three role-specific field sections

STEP 5: Fill & Submit Form
  Fill required fields for all selected roles
  Click "Register"

STEP 6: Check Google Sheets
  Open: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/14gfIXPlZQGuYYAWiW1RHlcDlBf_Tm63JMRrJ-4pyqwk/
  
  Main Sheet: Should show roles as "job_seeker, mentor, trainer"
  Job_Seekers Sheet: Should have your entry
  Mentors Sheet: Should have your entry
  Trainers Sheet: Should have your entry
